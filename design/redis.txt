Channels
	roadblock/
		history - all roadblocks that were run during the lifetime of the message queue

		expired - a list of any roadblock(s) that hit their timeout 
			- this can be used as a hint to newly arrived members to exit prematurely -- whether it's the leader or the follower(s)
			- this is a subset of history

		closed - a list of roadblock(s) that were successfully complted
		       - this is probably just used for debugging purposes
		       - this is a subset of history
